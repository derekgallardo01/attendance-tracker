Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Overview

Attendance Tracker ("the App") is a Google Meet add-on that helps meeting organizers track participant attendance, export reports to Google Sheets, and optionally receive post-meeting summaries via email and Slack. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Data We Collect

  • Google account information: Your name, email address, and Google account/Workspace domain (obtained via Google Sign-In).
  • Meeting data: Conference IDs, participant names, join/leave times, and session counts (obtained via the Google Meet REST API).
  • Calendar data: Event titles, start/end times, and attendee lists for meetings you match to Meet calls (obtained via the Google Calendar API).
  • Authentication tokens: OAuth refresh tokens (encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM) to maintain your session.
  • User settings: If you configure a Slack incoming webhook URL for post-meeting digests, that URL is stored on your user settings document.
  • Product analytics: Anonymous pageview beacons on the marketing site (page path, referrer, UTM parameters, viewport width, user agent, IP). Not used for cross-site tracking.
  • Feedback submissions: If you use the in-product feedback widget, we store the message body, any email you provided, and context about where you were in the app.
  • Acquisition attribution: First-touch UTM parameters and referrer URL captured when you first visit the site or install the add-on, so we can understand how users find us.
  • Public share links: If you generate a share link for a recurring meeting series, the token, associated series identifier, and view count are stored. Share links expire after 30 days.

How We Use Your Data

  • Display real-time attendance in the Meet side panel.
  • Auto-export attendance reports to Google Sheets in your Google Drive when a meeting ends.
  • Send you a post-meeting digest email with the sheet link and attendance summary.
  • Post a post-meeting summary to your Slack channel (only if you've configured a Slack webhook).
  • Match meeting participants to calendar invitees for RSVP status and no-show tracking.
  • Roll up attendance across recurring meeting series and email you daily alerts when someone's attendance pattern changes significantly.
  • Send occasional re-engagement emails if you signed up and stopped using the product.

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties beyond the sub-processors listed below. We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

Sub-processors

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Google Cloud Platform (Firestore, Cloud Run, Cloud Scheduler, Secret Manager) — primary data storage and application hosting, US us-central1 region.
  • Resend (privacy policy) — transactional email delivery. Recipient email addresses and message content pass through Resend to be delivered. Delivery metadata (opens, bounces) is retained per Resend's policy.
  • Cloudflare (privacy policy) — CDN and DNS proxy for the marketing site. HTTP request metadata (IP address, user agent, requested URL) passes through Cloudflare's edge network.
  • Slack Technologies — only if you configure a Slack webhook. Post-meeting summaries are POSTed to the webhook URL you provide, which is under your control.
  • Sentry — application error tracking. Stack traces and error metadata may include limited request context; personal data is not intentionally captured.

Data Storage

  • Meeting and user data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore, isolated by your account's domain (personal Gmail users share the gmail.com tenant scope).
  • Refresh tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before storage.
  • All primary data is hosted on Google Cloud Platform in the US (us-central1 region).

Data Retention

  • Meeting attendance data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Pageview beacon data is retained for up to 90 days for product analytics.
  • Public share links auto-expire after 30 days.
  • When you uninstall the App, your data is deleted within 30 days.
  • You can request immediate data deletion by contacting us.

Google API Scopes

The App requests the following Google API scopes:

  • openid email profile — Sign you in and identify your account.
  • meetings.space.readonly — View participant information for meetings you have access to.
  • calendar.events.readonly — View calendar events to match attendees and RSVP status.
  • drive.file — Create a "Meet Attendance Tracker" folder and spreadsheet in your Drive. This scope only grants access to files the App creates; it does not access other files in your Drive.

Your Rights

  • You can revoke the App's access at any time via Google Account Permissions.
  • You can clear your Slack webhook at any time from the in-app Settings modal.
  • You can request export or deletion of your data by contacting us.

Security

We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS encryption in transit, encrypted tokens at rest, and Google Cloud's built-in security infrastructure.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll bump the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be highlighted on the site.
